Welcome to Graphic Era Deemed to be University

Graphic Era Deemed to be University is a deemed university located in Clement Town, Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. Graphic Era University was established in 1993 as Graphic Era Institute of Technology by Prof. Kamal Ghanshala. GEU is NAAC accredited with grade 'A' and is approved by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), University Grants Commission (UGC), All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE) and Association of Indian Universities (AIU). GEU is also an institutional member of the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE). GEU is a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities, London. GEU is also a member of the International Association of Universities (IAU), UNESCO, Paris. GEU is also a member of the Computer Society of India (CSI), Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), Indian Society of Lighting Engineers (ISLE), Indian Society for Training & Development (ISTD) and Indian Society of Geomatics (ISG).
